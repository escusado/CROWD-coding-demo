About us

80 Degrees West is an online interior design studio. We help you to design beautiful spaces that reflect your style. We do this in two ways:

Step One -You know those piles of ripped out magazine pages you’ve had lying around your home? Go digital and put them here. Get your thoughts organized using our free Design Notebook. Plan your project and collaborate with friends.

Step Two -Decorate! You can design-it-yourself or you can hire us to help you! Get the help you need, when you need it.

Features

  • You can organize your decorating projects:

    Your Design Notebook holds as many projects as you want, for as long as you want. And it is free! Keep all of your ideas in one place.

  • You can ask your friends and family for their opinion:

    Want to know what your stylish Aunt Ruth thinks of the sofa you want to buy? Ask her. Show her your options. Share your inspiration.

  • You can collaborate with a professional designer:

    Ever wanted to ask a designer for advice on your decorating project? For a reasonable price, we’ll work with you on small dilemmas or complete projects. You decide!
